About

Dr. Hifza Irfan Chaudhry is a sociologist whose work integrates academic scholarship, teaching, and applied social research. Her focus areas include road safety, sociology of law, criminology, and family and marriage studies.

She has more than 9 years of university-level teaching and research support experience, with strong expertise in surveys, in-depth interviews, FGDs, and qualitative content analysis.
